Why AI Matters

AI Changes How We Work

Artificial intelligence affects the types of tasks that workers perform, the skills employers expect, and the speed at which industries evolve. Brookings reports that AI will influence tasks in 0% of jobs in the United States.

Who Is Most at Risk?

AI benefits those with strong digital access and training, and leaves others behind.

Strong Access

  • More training options
  • Faster adaptation to tools
  • Better job mobility

Limited Access

  • Less training
  • Higher risk of disruption
  • More stress and uncertainty

The Research

Reports from Brookings, the White House, and the European Commission agree that training, literacy, and fair evaluation systems determine whether AI helps or harms workers.
