The Workforce Path Forward

AI does not automatically replace workers, but without planning it widens gaps. Here are the actions we should prioritize.

1. Expand Digital Access

Reliable internet and devices should be available in all communities.

2. Build AI and Data Training

Affordable courses through schools and workforce centers.

3. Ensure Fair AI Evaluation

Transparent systems for hiring and performance reviews.

4. Create Partnerships

Universities, employers, and community groups should align goals.

About the Author

My name is Angel G. Arroyo Buitrago, and I am a first generation Hispanic computer science student at the University of Maryland. Growing up without consistent digital access shaped my understanding of how technology can empower, or exclude, communities.

This project is a remediation of my academic position paper, transformed into a public facing website to spread awareness about AI's impact on workers.
