A public exploration of how artificial intelligence is reshaping jobs, skills, and opportunity, written by a first generation Hispanic computer science student in Maryland.

My Story

I am a first generation Hispanic computer science major at the University of Maryland. I learned coding slowly, often on borrowed or limited devices. AI helps me learn faster, but it also raises the stakes for everyone who never had equal access.

Technology can change a life, but only with the right tools, training, and support.

AI Is Changing the Workforce

Artificial intelligence shapes the job market that students and workersare entering right now. Tools like ChatGPT speed up work and expand possibility, but also create pressure and uncertainty.
